Types of Shapes

We can identify four types of Shapes on a clock:

  • Dial Shapes - They draw the dial's background. For example: DialShape and TextShape . The last one draws a text on the dial's background.
  • Angular Shapes - They are repeated at different angles around the clock, like the ticks (Tick1Shape and Tick5Shape) and the numbers (NumbersShape).
  • Hand Shapes - They draw the hands: HourHandShape, MinuteHandShape, SweepHandShape.
  • Top Shapes - Displayed on top of all other shapes. For example: PinShape that is drawing the pin that fixes the hands. It is the only one in this category right now, but in this category can apeare in the future shapes that will draw effects like glass reflections or some dirty glass cover.
